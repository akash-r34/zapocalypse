# Instagram Post Prompts — Zapocalypse Content Factory

Prompts for Gemini to write Instagram posts marketing Zapocalypse.
Each prompt targets a distinct feature and uses a different content angle.
Copy-paste any prompt directly into Gemini. Adjust the brand voice note to taste.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

Each prompt is self-contained. Paste it into Gemini (any tier).
Specify at the bottom of any prompt: `Output format: caption only, 150–220 words, Instagram-ready, include 5–8 hashtags at the end.`

## Post 6 — C2PA Content Credentials
**Feature:** C2PA manifests — provenance metadata, "Do Not Train" tag
**Angle:** Trust / EU AI Act compliance — the upcoming creator obligation

```
Write an Instagram caption for Zapocalypse, an AI content tool, about its content credential system based on the C2PA standard.

Context: The EU AI Act requires disclosure of AI-generated content starting August 2026. The C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity) standard is the industry's answer — a cryptographic way to attach "this was made with AI, by this creator, at this time" to any content.

Zapocalypse generates a C2PA manifest for every output automatically. It captures:
- Which AI model was used (e.g., gemini-2.5-flash)
- When it was generated (ISO timestamp)
- A SHA-256 content hash (proves nothing was altered after generation)
- A "Do Not Train" flag (opt-out of future AI training on your content)

The manifest travels with your content. It's verifiable proof — not a watermark that can be cropped, but a cryptographic record attached to the output.

This is built in. You don't opt into it. Every run, every platform, every output gets credentials.

Angle: Frame this as "the thing responsible creators will wish they'd started earlier." Not fear-mongering but calm inevitability — this is where the industry is heading. Tone: credible, grounded, appeals to professional creators and brand managers who care about compliance and trust.

Output: Instagram caption, 160–200 words, 6 hashtags. Mix of AI ethics, creator tools, compliance hashtags.
```

## Notes on Using Gemini for These Prompts

- **Model:** Gemini 2.5 Flash is sufficient for Instagram copy. Gemini 2.5 Pro if you want more nuanced tone calibration.
- **If output is too long:** Add "Keep it under 200 words, cut any sentence that doesn't add new information."
- **If output sounds generic:** Add "Avoid: 'In today's fast-paced world', 'game-changer', 'revolutionize', 'leverage'. If you find yourself writing any of these, replace with a concrete example instead."
- **For carousel posts (9-slide Instagram):** Append "Reformat as 9 Instagram carousel slides. Each slide: 1 headline (max 8 words) + 1–2 supporting sentences. Slide 1 = hook. Slide 9 = CTA."
- **For Reels scripts:** Append "Reformat as a 30-second Reels script: hook (0–3s), problem (3–10s), solution (10–22s), CTA (22–30s). Include suggested b-roll or screen recording notes per section."
